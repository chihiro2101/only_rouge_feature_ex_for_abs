archaeological ruins and ancient texts show that handshaking – also known as dexiosis – was practiced in ancient greece as far back as the 5th century bc ; a depiction of two soldiers shaking hands can be found on part of a 5th-century bc funerary stele on display in the pergamon museum , berlin ( stele sk1708 ) and other funerary steles like the one of the 4th century bc which depicts thraseas and his wife euandria handshaking in switzerland , it may be expected to shake the women 's hands first another version popular with politicians is a '' photo-op handshake '' in which , after the initial grasp both individuals turn to face present photographers and camera men and stay this way for several seconds on 21 september 2009 , jack tsonis and lindsay morrison then broke that record by shaking hands for 12 hours , 34 minutes and 56 seconds
