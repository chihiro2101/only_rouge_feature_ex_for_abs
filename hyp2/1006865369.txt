tottenham court road runs from euston road in the north , to st giles circus ( the junction of oxford street and charing cross road ) at its southern end south of torrington place the road marks the traditional boundary of the parishes of st pancras , london to the west , and st giles to the east ( due to long-standing shared administrative arrangements , st giles is often described as a part of bloomsbury ) the manor is mentioned in the domesday book as belonging to the dean and chapter of st paul 's cathedral the kinks reference the road in their song denmark street ( 1970 )
