einar gerhardsen was born in the municipality of asker , in the county of akershus in 1918 , during the finnish civil war , gerhardsen resigned his membership of the church of norway after the church sided with the '' whites '' against the '' reds '' he was convicted several times of taking part in subversive activities until he , along with the rest of the labour party , gradually moved from communism to democratic socialism by the mid-1930s , labour was a major force on the national political scene , becoming the party of government under prime minister johan nygaardsvold from 1935 until the nazi invasion in 1940 after the german occupation of norway in 1940 , gerhardsen became acting chairman of the labour party , as the chairman , oscar torp had gone into exile during world war ii , gerhardsen took part in the organised resistance against the german occupation of norway , and was arrested on 11 september 1941 after the war , gerhardsen formed the interim government which sat from the end of the occupation in may 1945 until the general election held in october the same year in foreign policy , he aligned norway with the western powers at the end of the 1940s after some initial hesitation within the governing party , and norway became a founding member of nato in the aftermath , the gerhardsen government was accused of not complying with laws enacted by parliament although this new government lasted only three weeks , until the socialist people 's party realigned itself with labour , it formed the basis for an opposition victory under the leadership of per borten at the 1965 general election gerhardsen 's political legacy is still an important force in norwegian politics , especially within his own party , jens stoltenberg : vi bygger landet speech april 22 , 2010 , office of the prime minister , retrieved 18 september 2012
