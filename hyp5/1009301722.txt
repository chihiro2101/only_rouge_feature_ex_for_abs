during the second world war , the kerch peninsula became the scene of much desperate combat between forces of the soviet red army and nazi germany the cable railway ( aerial tramway ) , which went into operation on 14 june 1943 with a daily capacity of one thousand tons , was only adequate for the defensive needs of the seventeenth army in the kuban bridgehead because of frequent earth tremors , this bridge would have required vast quantities of extra-strength steel girders , and their transport would have curtailed shipments of military material to the crimea the bridge was never completed , and the wehrmacht finished evacuating the kuban bridgehead in september 1943
