from the mid-20th century electronic devices called '' keyers '' have been developed , operated by special keys of various designs generally categorized as single-paddle keys and '' iambic '' or double-paddle keys with a dual paddle both contacts may be closed simultaneously , enabling the '' iambic '' functions of an electronic keyer that is designed to support them often , these were simple '' strap '' keys , in which a bend in the key lever provided the key 's spring action however , electronic keyers ( single-paddle or iambic ) will produce '' perfect '' code at a set speed , thus only inter-character and inter-word spacing can produce a semblance of a fist .
