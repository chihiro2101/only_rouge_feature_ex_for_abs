the brigade of franz josef de lusignan was advancing to the north of monte baldo ; and the troops of peter vitus von quosdanovich and josef philipp vukassovich were pouring down the roads on either side of the adige before daybreak as the french were moving on the road from rivoli to incanale joubert attacked and drove the austrians from the chapel of san marco the fire of his guns and the pressure from quosdanovich forced the french out of the village of osteria and onto the rivoli plateau making the best of interior lines and his advantage in artillery , bonaparte thinned out joubert 's lines facing the austrians frontally at the trambasore heights as much as possible and concentrated them before the gorge the battle of rivoli was bonaparte 's greatest victory at the time on 13 january his corps ( 9,000 men ) had crossed north of legnano and driven straight for the relief of mantua which was besieged by french forces under jean sérurier the austrians were attacked from the front by masséna ( who had force marched from rivoli ) and from the rear by the division of pierre augereau , and were thus forced to surrender the entire force the remainder were sent to austria after swearing an oath to not serve against the french for a year , 1,500 guns were found in the fortress he prepared one last campaign to the east , into the heartland of austria to the gates of vienna itself
