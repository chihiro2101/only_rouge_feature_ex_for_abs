circulars are composed of two pointed , straight tips connected by a flexible cable and may be used for both knitting flat or knitting in the round if older circulars with the nylon or plastic cables are coiled for storage it may be necessary to soak them in hot water for a few minutes to get them to uncoil and relax for ease of use a hook gauge makes it possible to determine the size of a knitting needle there is an '' old us system '' that is divided into standard and steel needles , the latter being fine lace needles
