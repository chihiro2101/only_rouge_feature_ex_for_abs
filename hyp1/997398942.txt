the most widely recognized form of needle is the single-pointed needle it is a slender , straight stick tapered to a point at one end , with a knob at the other end to prevent stitches from slipping off such needles are always used in pairs and are usually 10-16 inches ( 25.4–40.6 & nbsp ; cm ) long but , due to the compressibility of knitted fabrics , may be used to knit pieces significantly wider the knitting of new stitches occurs only at the tapered ends fictional depictions of knitting in films , television programs , animation , and comic strips almost always show knitting done on straight needles the oldest type of needle is the straight double-pointed needle double-pointed needles are somewhat shorter than single-pointed or circular needles , and are usually used in the 13–20 & nbsp ; cm length range , although they are also made longer a cable needle is a special type of double-pointed needle that is typically very short and used to hold a very small number of stitches temporarily while the knitter is forming a cable pattern they are often u-shaped , or have a u-shaped bend , to keep the held stitches from falling off while the primary needle is being used the first us patent for a circular needle was issued in 1918 , although in europe they may have been used a little earlier julia knitted a tension square of ten stitches and ten rows in stocking stitch using knitting needles that were 6.5 & nbsp ; cm in diameter and 3.5 metres long circular needles may be stored with the cables coiled in cases made specifically for this purpose or hung dangling from a hanger device with cables straight there are holes of various sizes through which the needles are passed to determine which hole they fit best , and often a ruler along the edge for determining the tension ( also called gauge ) of a sample the principles of knitting : methods and techniques of hand knitting
