as an assistant to many of the sculptors in chicago , for their part in constructing the '' white city '' for the world 's columbian exposition during the next decade , he worked with other established sculptors : augustus saint-gaudens , philip martiny , alexander phimister proctor , john william kitson and others his work at the louisiana purchase exposition , under karl bitter , the foremost architectural sculptor of the time , allowed lawrie to develop both his skills and his reputation as an architectural sculptor he was an instructor in yale 's school of fine arts from 1908 to 1919 and taught in the architecture program at harvard university from 1910 to 1912 he was a member of the national institute of arts and letters , the american academy of arts and letters , the national academy of design , and the architectural league of new york among his many awards was the aia gold medal of the american institute of architects in 1921 and 1927 , a medal of honor from the architectural league of new york in 1931 , and an honorary degree from yale university commission of fine arts in washington , dc from 1933 to 1937 and again from 1945 to 1950 ; it oversees federal
